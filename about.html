About Me

Brand
Architect

"Brand Architect at your command , each project is a brick."

I'm Hiu (Nguyễn Quốc Hiếu), a brand architect turning complex brand challenges into elegant campaign systems. Each project is a brick, laid over years of building campaigns and refining ideas that shape my path toward brand architecture.

I specialize in working closely with clients across FMCG, fintech, and tech , blending strategy with modern culture to bring brands to life through impactful, human-centric ideas that drive measurable results.

My work spans from decoding a brief to designing the full campaign system , from brand strategy and creative platform to 360° execution across media, social, and offline.

I've had the privilege of working on brands like Samsung, Tiger Beer, GreenFarm by Vinamilk, Berocca, KFC, ACB, and Visa , building campaigns that land culturally and deliver business results.

When I'm not building brand architecture, I'm wandering , a Hiuflaneur at heart.
